WELCOME TO THE COSMO LIBRARY

For research, inspiration and enjoyment.

The Cosmo Library is one of the world's great lending libraries, with an amazing collection of over 1 million books, and an equally extraordinary history. This extensive collection includes works on individual countries, monarchs, historical events and topics such as feudalism, international relations and chivalry.
